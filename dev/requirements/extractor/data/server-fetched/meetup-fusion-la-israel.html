Super Organizer

Fusion VC - Israel

Details

​Fusion VC, Google for Startups, Poalim Tech and AI Builders Israel invite you to a special morning event focused on one of the biggest shifts happening in the startup ecosystem right now:
​The rise of AI-native builders, solo founders, vibe coders, and lean startup teams building faster than ever before.
​As AI tools reshape product development, distribution, and company building, the traditional venture capital playbook is evolving as well.

## ​RSVP HERE

  • ​What does the next generation of startups look like?
  • ​What are investors looking for in 2026?
  • ​Can solo founders build venture-scale companies?
  • ​And how should founders think about fundraising, velocity, and execution in the AI era?

​Join leading founders, operators, and investors for an honest discussion about the future of building and investing.

## ​What We’ll Cover:

  • ​The new pre-seed benchmarks in the AI era
  • ​How AI-native startups are built differently
  • ​Solo founders vs. traditional teams
  • ​The rise of vibe coding and ultra-lean execution
  • ​What top VCs are looking for in 2026
  • ​How founders can move faster with AI tools
  • ​Real lessons from builders already scaling AI products
