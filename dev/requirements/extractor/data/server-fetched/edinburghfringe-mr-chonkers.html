Performer / Performance company

John Norris

John Norris (Mr Chonkers, allegedly) is trying out 1-3 hours of new stuff. Goofs, gags, musings, bits and vague energy. Nonsense. If you're the type of person that needs more information before coming to a show, well, this is not for you. Stay far away, my friend. Save yourself. No press allowed. No awards judges allowed.
